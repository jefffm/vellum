# ADR 0023: Prioritize musical proofs over assurance machinery

## Status

Accepted — Owner approved the aggressive product pivot on 2026-07-16.

## Context

The Instrument Intelligence specification grew to 2,425 lines and 107 tracer bullets. Its
execution path placed publication trust, authority inventories, evidence receipts, migration,
sealed evaluation, qualification, and release adjudication ahead of the first convincing
instrument-specific arrangement. That level of assurance is disproportionate to Vellum's
current local-first, single-Owner, research-quality stage.

The durable product need remains: source-backed musical understanding and idiomatic,
mechanically plausible arrangements for five-course baroque guitar, thirteen-course baroque
lute, and six-string classical guitar.

## Decision

Vellum will optimize the next program for three end-to-end musical proofs. Infrastructure may
enter the critical path only when an immediate product vertical requires it.

For local Owner-reviewed knowledge, a versioned record with authority lane, applicability,
citation, review state, and explicit activation is sufficient. The immutable and authority-lane
principles of ADR 0022 remain. ADR 0022's external attestation verification, advisory
verification, complete registry inventory, Merkle publication, transactional cutover, and
general migration requirements are deferred until Vellum actually accepts independently
authored or distributed Knowledge Pack releases.

Evaluation retains versioned cases, separate dimensions, hard-gate honesty, private holdout
identity, and reviewed playtest evidence from ADRs 0020 and 0021. Encrypted Vaults, sealed
evaluator processes, append-only qualification ledgers, curator role systems, and formal
Machine/Release Complete adjudication are deferred until hidden-evaluation use or external
capability claims make them necessary.

The old 107-tracer execution graph, triple-ref bootstrap, GraphQL publication observation,
authority-path digest cascade, clause ledger, and per-tracer receipt protocol are frozen
historical work. Ordinary commits, pushes, focused tests, and the repository quality gates
govern the replacement program.

No migration framework is required without deployed data that must be migrated. Compatibility
adapters may remain where they already protect real records; speculative legacy envelopes do
not block product work.

## Relationship to existing decisions

ADRs 0001 through 0019 and 0021 continue to govern their musical, local-first, versioning,
source-truth, search, and reviewed-learning concerns.

This decision narrows the present applicability of ADRs 0020 and 0022. It does not reject their
high-assurance mechanisms; it requires a demonstrated trigger before they become mandatory.

## Consequences

- Instrument compilers, source-backed idioms, real PDF workflow, editing, playback, and musical
  evaluation become the critical path.
- Previously implemented provenance and authority isolation may remain, but agents must not
  deepen it unless a current vertical needs it.
- Product completion is established by the three musical proofs and one late Owner playtest,
  not by a certification ceremony.
- Future multi-author publication or externally claimed qualification will require a new ADR
  that names the concrete threat, users, data, and interoperability boundary.
