Status: ready-for-agent

## Outcome

Turn the accepted three-target prototype baseline into genuinely idiomatic phrase-level realization for five-course baroque guitar, thirteen-course baroque lute, and six-string classical guitar. The three targets are coequal. Shared contracts must support each target without flattening its distinct technique, notation, texture, or evaluation semantics.

## Boundary inherited from the prototype closeout

The predecessor wave established source lineage, Analysis, Briefs and Plans, exact Instrument Instances, bounded search, notation, playback, workbench interaction, and evaluation infrastructure. It did not establish calibrated physical playability, historical authority, or coherent multi-voice realization. The Owner accepted that boundary on 2026-07-13 and supplied three exact counterexamples:

- baroque guitar: a nominally playable Greensleeves result that is neither idiomatic punteado nor valid mixed-style alfabeto writing;
- baroque lute: measure 3 uses `f/b`, an unacceptable fret-1-to-fret-5 span on the Owner's 690 mm lute despite closer equivalent realizations; and
- classical guitar: the Principal Voice survives but the source's 59-note bass becomes four isolated events rather than a coherent target bass.
