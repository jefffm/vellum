# 02 — Shared phrase and voice obligations

Status: ready-for-agent

Type: AFK

Blocked by: 01

## What to build

Carry the baseline source's voice identity, activity, rests, phrase contour, cadence, harmonic
function, and preservation obligations through analysis, planning, candidate search, score,
audit, UI explanation, and evaluation. The narrow vertical must support a Principal Voice and
at least one coherent subordinate voice without assuming that every source has a permanent
melody.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Source Voice Graph evidence distinguishes voice identity from staff and pitch height.
- [ ] Target Voice, Harmonic, and Relationship Plans express phrase-level obligations used by search.
- [ ] A selected candidate preserves the Principal Voice under Faithful Reduction or fails explicitly.
- [ ] A subordinate voice cannot disappear or jump without an explicit planned transformation.
- [ ] The arrangement view explains preserved, transformed, omitted, and generated material at score locations.
- [ ] Mutations of phrase timing, cadence, or voice continuity fail the focused evaluation.

## Gates

Base gates plus affected `eval:*` suites.
