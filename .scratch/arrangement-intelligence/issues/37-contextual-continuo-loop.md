# Contextual continuo through the shared loop

Status: complete

Type: AFK

User stories: U3, U6, U8

## What to build

Validate the shared architecture with a non-position-search domain that realizes connected continuo voices under exact historical and bass authority.

## Acceptance criteria

- [x] The plan distinguishes complete realization, separate bass, and honest reduction.
- [x] Unfigured and figured bass use contextual harmony, alterations, voice leading, and prepared resolutions.
- [x] Generated voices remain distinct from source evidence and satisfy complete audit and playback semantics.
- [x] Cross-domain Evaluation Cases reuse shared contracts without flattening continuo state.

## Delivered

- Three sibling contextual-continuo Evaluation Cases exercise a complete keyboard realization, baroque guitar with a named separate bass, and an explicitly labeled Continuo Reduction under idiomatic-adaptation policy.
- Each case reuses the shared Source Truth, Performance Brief, proportional Plan, Arrangement Family, Search, Transformation Report, Preservation Audit, deliverable, Evaluation Run, Case Run, and Card contracts.
- The domain retains exact Realization Profile, Continuo Foundation, figures, harmonic context, generated-voice lineage, candidate strategy, prepared dissonance/resolution, and bass authority. A diagnostic explicitly prohibits fretted-position state from becoming continuo domain truth.
- Unfigured bass now creates a traceable key-contextual implied 5-3 realization request. Authored figured bass continues to preserve its explicit intervals and alterations.
- The full and separate-bass cases preserve the reviewed prepared 4-3 suspension. Honest reduction discloses the unsounded foundation and does not falsely claim that the complete prepared relationship survived.
- Continuo Reduction engraving now projects principal and generated voices, retains source figures as evidence, omits the unsounded foundation from playback, and visibly labels the reduction instead of crashing or implying completeness.
- All three cases compile notation, PDF, SVG, canonical MIDI, and semantic Audio Preview, then emit Cards whose deterministic hard gates pass while historical, physical, and Owner authority remain incomplete.
- `authoritative_with_disclosed_uncertainty` now correctly passes the Source Authority gate while retaining the disclosure; only review-required or best-effort-only truth fails authorization.

## Verification

- Unit tests cover explicit figures, alterations, prepared resolution, implied unfigured 5-3 requests, separate bass, honest reduction, and infeasible-target Plan Conflict.
- The cross-domain harness test verifies all three Plan/disposition branches, generated/source role separation, complete audits, reduction disclosure, compiled projections, playback parts, shared Card dimensions, and continuo-specific diagnostics.
- The existing HTTP end-to-end tracer continues to verify durable compile/audio deliverables and MIDI event counts.
- Full gates and dependency hashes are recorded in `evidence/T37/verification.json`.

## Honest limits

- The implied unfigured-bass default is a profile-scoped 5-3 request, not a claim that all historical unfigured-bass practice is reducible to root-position triads. Broader style/profile inference remains reviewable domain policy.
- A Continuo Reduction is accepted only as a disclosed idiomatic adaptation; it does not satisfy complete-foundation fidelity and does not inherit the complete realization's prepared-resolution claim.
- Machine validation does not establish historical appropriateness, physical playability, audible quality, or Owner usefulness; those remain scoped human evidence.
