Status: ready-for-agent

Type: AFK

User stories: U7, U9

## What to build

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Evidence roles separate fitting, development, held-out evaluation, and monitoring.
- [ ] Calibration Candidates retain supporting and conflicting evidence.
- [ ] Personal Defaults, ergonomic changes, Knowledge Candidates, and fixtures use their existing approval boundaries.
- [ ] An example cannot both fit an evaluator and prove its generalization.
