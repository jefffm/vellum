# Initial three-target parity candidate workflow

Status: complete

Type: AFK

User stories: U1, U2, U3, U4, U5, U6, U7, U8, U9

## What to build

Run one complete source family through all three priority targets and prepare objective evidence for later physical approval.

## Acceptance criteria

- [x] All targets share exact source truth and family planning while searching independently.
- [x] Each target produces appropriate notation, playback, audits, Evaluation Cards, and workbench interaction.
- [x] No target remains on first-fit or proxy-labeled intelligence.
- [x] Physical and specialist evidence remains explicitly awaiting rather than assumed.

## Delivered

- One Greensleeves source lineage is imported once, then realized as three sibling arrangements in one Arrangement Family for baroque guitar, 13-course lute, and classical guitar.
- The siblings share the exact source digest, Normalized Score, Analysis Record, and target-portable family decisions while retaining distinct target Plans, bounded Arrangement Searches, candidates, selected scores, and exact Instrument Instances.
- Every target search evaluates multiple candidates with the policy-lexicographic comparison contract; no first-fit or proxy-labeled path qualifies for parity.
- Baroque guitar and lute project French tablature; classical guitar projects standard notation. Every target persists LilyPond, sanitized browser SVG, PDF, canonical MIDI, and event-identity Audio Preview deliverables.
- The complete saved projection set is the workbench boundary for notation display, playback, selection/prompt interaction, and versioned editing already provided by the shared Arrangement Score workflow.
- Three immutable Case Runs and Evaluation Cards separately report source authority, preservation/transformation, Plan realization, mechanics, history/Analysis, notation, playback, workflow/recovery, human/physical evidence, and Owner usefulness.
- Deterministic hard gates, projections, and workflow evidence pass. Historical authority, physical playability, editorial quality, audible quality, and Owner usefulness remain incomplete/unknown and are explicitly `awaiting_human` for the late review tracers.
- `eval:parity` executes the complete candidate workflow and emits machine-readable evidence.

## Verification

- The parity test proves common source/Analysis/family identity, equal portable decisions, three independent searches/Plans/scores, multiple evaluated candidates, exact target layouts, five persisted deliverables per target, complete Card dimension families, and no overall grade.
- The same test reloads all three Case Runs and checks the independent-search diagnostic and absence of first-fit/proxy labeling.
- Full gates and dependency hashes are recorded in `evidence/T36/verification.json`.

## Honest limits

- This tracer establishes an objective parity candidate, not human acceptance. Cards intentionally remain incomplete because physical and specialist evidence has not occurred.
- Successful LilyPond/MIDI projection proves semantic and infrastructure contracts, not visual editorial quality, audible musical quality, or physical comfort.
- The workbench uses the shared interaction contract already exercised by selection, prompt, playback-follow, and versioned-edit tracers; T41-T44 retain target-specific and Owner acceptance rather than being inferred here.
