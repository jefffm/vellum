## What to build

Under separate qualified roles, commit the legal eligible pool and reviewed truth before any candidate output, freeze the system, and allow the sealed runner to execute automatically through an honest Machine Complete judgment.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Independent curator precommits eligible pool, rights, contamination closures, coverage, output-independent invalid-fixture policy, reserve selection, exhaustion, role conflicts, ledger genesis, and inherited state in the Vault.
- [ ] Independent scope-qualified truth reviewers approve exact truth/gates; conflicts, credentials, scope, freshness, revocation, and unresolved disagreements are recorded separately from curator/evaluator/calibrator/operator.
- [ ] Compiler, packs, policies, manifests, evaluator, execution policy, provider/runtime, and performance profile freeze before the first output.
- [ ] Runner attempts at least two non-Greensleeves groups per target plus dedicated Continuo and imitative groups, retaining every attempt and inheriting every valid failure/reserve cursor after repair.
- [ ] Public output contains only opaque/redacted Cards, exact Claim Scopes, compatibility/provider validity, release-floor status, and Machine Complete or precise noncomplete reasons.
